Summary

Inception is a science-fiction thriller about a skilled thief who enters people's dreams to steal or plant ideas. Dom Cobb is offered a chance to clear his record if he can successfully perform inception on a target.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, Ken Watanabe as Saito.
