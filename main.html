Summary

Inception is a science-fiction thriller that explores the art of entering and manipulating dreams. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, is offered a chance to have his past crimes forgiven if he can plant an idea into someone’s mind—a process known as inception.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.
